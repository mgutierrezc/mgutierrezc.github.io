# *Ongoing Personal Projects*

## Effects of Collateral Institutions on Lending

<em>With Kevin McCabe and Maggie Lehr</em>

This is a paper-in-process born from the GMU/IFREE workshop on Computational Experimental Economics at GMU. Using a MTurk online experiment and taking the Trust Game as our framework, we study the effects of different characteristics of a collateral institution on lending, e.g. the presence of contract enforcement, loan productivity, salvage fee and under an endogenous rate of return.

## Studying the effect of collateral institutions on lending with human subjects

<em>With Peter Banks and Nicholas McFaden</em>

This is a poster presentation of a paper-in-process currently co-authored by PhD Kevin McCabe, PhD(c) Maggie Lehr and me. However, the co-authorship of this poster has been given to all the GMU Summer Research Group in charge of the programming the experimental design: Peter Banks, Marco Gutierrez and Nicholas McFaden. We did an online experiment using MTurk to evaluate the behavior of lenders and borrowers under a collateral institution in the context of a Trust Game with differences accross a collateral salvage fee and contract enforcement.

Presented at Bogotá Experimental Economics Conference 2020 (Bogotá, Colombia) and at OSCAR poster presentation 2019 at George Mason University

## Policies for Transactional De-Dollarization: A Laboratory Study (November, 2019 to January, 2020)

<em>Authors: Johar Arrieta, David Florian-Hoyle, Kristian Lopez-Vargas and Valeria Morales</em>

This is an ongoing experimental study of the impact of three policies on the acceptance rate of foreign currency in transactions:
<br>
1. Taxes on transactions in foreign currency among domestic agents.
1. Storage costs for foreign currency.
1. Information on the acceptability of the foreign currency.<br>

As the *research assistant* of this project, I was in charge of adapting its code for online deployment, supervised the experimental sessions, which took place online and on the offices of the Peruvian Central Reserve Bank, developed alongside Kristian a protocol for online experiments for the [LEEX-PUCP](https://investigacion.pucp.edu.pe/grupos/gece/proyecto/leex/), the first experimental economics laboratory of my country.
